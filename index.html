Second semester hit, and I upgraded from "I can do everything" to "why is she not interested?" Spoiler: still no rizz. But hey, I made my first female friend! Third semester: plot twist—our section got divided, and so did my tiny group of friends. Enter Giftson, my black friend, who expanded my social circle faster than my GPA was increasing.
